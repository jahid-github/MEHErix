Pilot Project in Action: Customer Churn Prediction

Here’s a tangible example of how a short pilot can deliver immense value.

The Problem

A retail client was losing customers but didn't know who was about to leave or why. This made retention efforts expensive and ineffective.

Our Pilot Solution

In 5 weeks, we analyzed their sales data and built a machine learning model to predict which customers had a high probability of churning within the next 30 days.

The client can now proactively target at-risk customers with special offers, reducing churn by an estimated 15% and significantly boosting lifetime customer value.
